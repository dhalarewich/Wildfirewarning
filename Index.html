West Penticton Wildfire Warning System

The West Penticton Wildfire Warning System is a volunteer-based initiative aimed at providing the earliest possible warning of wildfire threats to our local communities. Our system is designed to detect and disseminate wildfire information swiftly and accurately to ensure the safety of West Penticton residents.

As a dedicated team of volunteers, we operate around the clock, monitoring various channels and sources for any signs of wildfire. In the event of a wildfire threat, we coordinate with local authorities to provide timely updates and instructions for community safety.

Our efforts are fueled by the commitment to protect our homes, families, and natural surroundings from the devastating impact of wildfires. We believe that through vigilance and proactive measures, we can make a significant difference in wildfire preparedness and response.

We invite members of the community to join us in our mission to enhance wildfire safety. Through education, awareness, and active participation, we can strengthen our defense against the threat of wildfires and foster a culture of preparedness that benefits all residents of West Penticton.
